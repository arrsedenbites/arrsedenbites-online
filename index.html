Our Story

A fresh new beginning for street food lovers.

Welcome to ARR's Eden Bites

We are a brand-new food cart with a big dream — to serve the most irresistible street food that feels like a bite of paradise. Our journey has just begun, fueled by a passion for bold flavors and the joy of sharing good food.

Every item on our menu is handcrafted with fresh ingredients, a dash of creativity, and a whole lot of heart. From the nostalgic Egg Kulfi to our signature Chicken Kababs and loaded French Fries, we promise an experience that's anything but ordinary.

Come say hello and be part of our story from the very first bite.
